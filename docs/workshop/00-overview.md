# Introduction
- Talk - Developer's Dilemma and why this workshop is important!

## Goals and Metrics for Workshop
### Goal
1. Pique interest for AI-Driven development. Experience it as interesting, fun and productive.
2. Increase skills with your chosen tool.
3. Find applications in current project: better ways of getting things done with AI.

### Non-goal
- Make you into an AI-dev expert. Only practice can do that!
- Use of AI outside of software product development. We want focus.

### Metrics:
- Participants work with AI support tomorrow -> North star!
- Participants increasingly work with AI support, exchange tips, influence others.

### Company goals:
Goal tracking Excel

## How we'll work
Repeat following...

### Teaching
Motivation, demo, tips from the front (e.g. 15 Min).

### Hands-on
Apply it in your project using inspirational ideas file (e.g. 1 hour).
Stay in room or split up.
Take your own breaks as necessary. No group breaks (except lunch).

### Gather and share
Let me know beforehand if you have something to share with the group.
Come together and enjoy a couple of demos from participants (e.g. 10 Min).
